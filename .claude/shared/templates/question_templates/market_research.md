# Market Research Template

Use this template when user asks about market analysis, industry trends, or competitive landscape.

## Template Structure

### TASK
Conduct comprehensive market research on [MARKET/INDUSTRY] to understand [SPECIFIC ASPECT: size, growth, trends, competition, opportunities].

### CONTEXT/BACKGROUND
This research will be used by [AUDIENCE: executives, investors, product team] to [PURPOSE: inform strategy, evaluate opportunities, assess competition].

### SPECIFIC QUESTIONS OR SUBTASKS
1. What is the current market size and historical growth (past 3-5 years)?
2. What are the projected growth rates and market forecasts (next 3-5 years)?
3. Who are the key players and what are their market shares?
4. What are the main market segments and their characteristics?
5. What are the key trends and drivers shaping the market?
6. What are the barriers to entry and competitive dynamics?
7. What are the emerging opportunities and threats?

### KEYWORDS
- [Market name] market size
- [Market name] growth rate CAGR
- [Market name] market forecast
- [Market name] competitive landscape
- [Market name] market trends
- [Market name] market segmentation
- Key players in [market name]

### CONSTRAINTS
- **Timeframe**: Focus on [YEARS, e.g., 2020-2025]
- **Geography**: [Global / Regional: North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, etc.]
- **Source Quality**: Prefer industry analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester, IDC, McKinsey) and market research firms
- **Data Recency**: Prioritize sources from past 2 years
- **Report Length**: [Comprehensive: 30-50 pages / Standard: 15-25 pages / Executive summary: 5-10 pages]

### OUTPUT FORMAT
- **Executive Summary**: 2-page overview with key findings
- **Full Report**: Detailed analysis with sections for each question
- **Data Visualizations**: Descriptions of charts for market size, growth, segmentation
- **Sources**: Bibliography with A-E quality ratings
- **Citation Style**: Inline citations with (Author/Org, Year) format

---

## Example Usage

**User Input**: "Research the AI in healthcare market"

**Refined Prompt**:

### TASK
Conduct comprehensive market research on the AI in healthcare market to understand current size, growth trajectory, key players, and emerging trends.

### CONTEXT/BACKGROUND
This research will inform investment decisions for a venture capital firm evaluating opportunities in healthcare AI startups.

### SPECIFIC QUESTIONS OR SUBTASKS
1. What is the current global AI in healthcare market size (2023-2024)?
2. What are the projected growth rates (CAGR) through 2030?
3. Who are the leading companies and what are their market positions?
4. What are the main application segments (diagnostics, drug discovery, patient monitoring, etc.)?
5. What are the key technological and regulatory trends?
6. What are the adoption barriers and success factors?
7. What are the most promising investment opportunities?

### KEYWORDS
- AI healthcare market size
- Artificial intelligence medical diagnosis
- Healthcare AI CAGR forecast
- Clinical AI adoption rates
- Medical AI companies
- Healthcare AI investment trends

### CONSTRAINTS
- **Timeframe**: 2020-2030 (historical and forecast)
- **Geography**: Global with regional breakdowns
- **Source Quality**: Industry analysts (B-rated), peer-reviewed studies (A-rated), reputable news (C-rated)
- **Data Recency**: 2022-2024 preferred
- **Report Length**: Comprehensive (30-40 pages)

### OUTPUT FORMAT
- Executive Summary: 2 pages
- Full Report: 30-40 pages with data tables
- Market size charts and segment breakdowns
- Bibliography with quality ratings
- Inline citations throughout
