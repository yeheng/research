# Competitive Analysis Template

Use this template when user asks to analyze competitors, competitive landscape, or market positioning.

## Template Structure

### TASK
Conduct competitive analysis of [COMPANY/PRODUCT] and its competitors in [MARKET/CATEGORY] to understand positioning, strengths, weaknesses, and strategic opportunities.

### CONTEXT/BACKGROUND
This analysis will inform [PURPOSE: product strategy, go-to-market planning, investment decision] for [AUDIENCE: product team, executives, investors].

### SPECIFIC QUESTIONS OR SUBTASKS
1. Who are the direct and indirect competitors?
2. What are each competitor's key products/services and features?
3. How do competitors position themselves (pricing, target market, value proposition)?
4. What are the strengths and weaknesses of each competitor?
5. What are the market shares and growth trajectories?
6. What are the competitive advantages and moats?
7. What are the strategic opportunities and threats?

### KEYWORDS
- [Company] competitors
- [Market] competitive landscape
- [Company] vs [Competitor] comparison
- [Market] market share
- [Company] competitive advantage
- [Product category] alternatives

### CONSTRAINTS
- **Scope**: [Direct competitors only / Include indirect competitors and substitutes]
- **Geography**: [Global / Specific regions]
- **Depth**: [High-level overview / Deep dive with financials]
- **Source Quality**: Company websites (C-rated), analyst reports (B-rated), financial filings (A-rated)
- **Recency**: Past 1-2 years (competitive landscape changes quickly)
- **Report Length**: [Concise: 15-20 pages / Comprehensive: 30-40 pages]

### OUTPUT FORMAT
- **Competitive Matrix**: Feature/capability comparison table
- **SWOT Analysis**: For each major competitor
- **Market Positioning Map**: Visual representation of competitive positioning
- **Strategic Recommendations**: Opportunities and threats
- **Company Profiles**: Detailed profiles of top 3-5 competitors
- **Citation Style**: Inline citations with company sources and analyst reports

---

## Example Usage

**User Input**: "Analyze competitors for Notion"

**Refined Prompt**:

### TASK
Conduct competitive analysis of Notion and its competitors in the productivity/collaboration software market to understand positioning, feature differentiation, and strategic opportunities.

### CONTEXT/BACKGROUND
This analysis will inform product strategy for a startup building in the productivity space and help identify differentiation opportunities.

### SPECIFIC QUESTIONS OR SUBTASKS
1. Who are Notion's direct competitors (Confluence, Coda, Roam Research, etc.)?
2. How do features compare (databases, wikis, collaboration, integrations)?
3. What are the pricing strategies and target markets for each?
4. What are the strengths and weaknesses of each platform?
5. What are the user adoption trends and market shares?
6. What are Notion's competitive advantages (flexibility, design, community)?
7. What are the gaps and opportunities in the market?

### KEYWORDS
- Notion competitors alternatives
- Notion vs Confluence Coda comparison
- Productivity software market
- Collaboration tools comparison
- Notion market share
- All-in-one workspace tools

### CONSTRAINTS
- **Scope**: Direct competitors (all-in-one workspace tools) and key indirect competitors (specialized tools)
- **Geography**: Global, with focus on North America and Europe
- **Depth**: Deep dive with feature comparison and user sentiment
- **Source Quality**: Company websites, product reviews (C-rated), analyst reports (B-rated), user forums
- **Recency**: 2023-2024 (product features change rapidly)
- **Report Length**: Comprehensive (30-35 pages)

### OUTPUT FORMAT
- Competitive matrix: Features, pricing, target users
- SWOT analysis: Notion and top 4 competitors
- Market positioning map: Flexibility vs. specialization, individual vs. enterprise
- User sentiment analysis: Reviews and feedback themes
- Strategic recommendations: Differentiation opportunities
- Inline citations with product documentation and reviews
